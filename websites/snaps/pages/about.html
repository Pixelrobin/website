About Us

Snaps Tours! was founded in 2002 with an aim to provide the best tour experiences avaliable for students and young adults, while keeping things at a manageable price. We started with a few tours in Europe, but have since grown to provide a total of 20 tours with locations all around the globe.

Over two million people have experienced other countries, learned other languages or participated in intercultural exchanges with Snaps! Tours. Our global network of suppliers, coupled with the largest volume of travelers in the industry, gives us the power to negotiate the best rates on flights, hotels, meals, and transportation—savings we pass along to you!

Our quality control staff travels year-round—inspecting hotels, evaluating tour directors, visiting sights—to ensure our exacting quality standards. No one else offers these prices for this level of quality.
